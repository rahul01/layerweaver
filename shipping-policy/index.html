Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Delivery Timeframes

We ship all orders directly from Pune, Maharashtra. Delivery times depend on your location.

🏙️

Pune

1–2 business days

🇮🇳

Rest of India

2–5 business days

📦 Orders are dispatched within 1 business day of confirmation. Custom or made-to-order products may take an additional 1–2 days to prepare before dispatch.

Free Shipping

We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, shipping is a flat ₹30 within Pune and ₹49 for the rest of India.

Shipping Partners

We ship via trusted courier partners including Delhivery, BlueDart, and India Post depending on your pincode and order size. You will receive a tracking link via WhatsApp or email once your order is dispatched.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number. You can use this to track your shipment on the courier partner's website. If you have not received a tracking update within 2 business days of your order, please reach out to us.

Delays & Issues

While we do our best to meet the delivery timelines above, occasional delays can happen due to weather, public holidays, or courier disruptions. If your shipment is significantly delayed or appears stuck:

  • WhatsApp us at +91 75587 83018 with your order ID.
  • Or email us at admin@layerweaver.com with the subject "Shipping Issue – [Order ID]".

We will investigate and get back to you within 1 business day.
