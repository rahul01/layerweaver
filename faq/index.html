Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, and our products.

What material are your products made from?

All our products are printed with PLA (Polylactic Acid), a plant-based, biodegradable plastic made from renewable resources like cornstarch. It's eco-friendly, non-toxic, and produces clean, detailed prints.

How long does delivery take?

Orders within Pune are typically delivered in 1-2 business days. Orders to the rest of India take 2-5 business days. You'll receive tracking details once your order is dispatched. See our Shipping Policy for full details.

Can I order a custom 3D print?

Yes! We offer on-demand 3D printing for custom items. Visit our On Demand Printing page or message us on WhatsApp with your idea, reference images, or STL file and we'll provide a quote.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery for items that arrive damaged or defective. Custom or personalised orders are non-returnable. See our Return and Exchange Policy for full details.

Do you ship outside India?

We currently ship within India only. Shipping is free on orders above ₹500. For orders below ₹500, a flat rate of ₹30 applies within Pune and ₹49 for the rest of India.

Do you offer 3D printing workshops?

Yes! We run 3D printing workshops for kids and adults in Pune. Sessions cover 3D design basics and hands-on printing. Visit our Workshops page to see upcoming sessions and enrol.
